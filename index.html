Welcome to the Reading Group on
Natural Language Processing at UO

The Natural Language Processing group at UO conducts research on Information Extraction, Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning and Machine Learning. We organize a reading group weekly in each quarter to discuss the recent advances in these areas. One or two papers will be presented in each meeting. We will also have a discussion section after each presentation to discuss about the topics. Everybody is welcome!

The time for the meeting will be announced at the beginning of each quarter. Please check this page regularly for the next papers to be presented and the location of the meeting.

This reading group is maintained by Prof. Thien Huu Nguyen. Come and talk with him if you want to do research!
